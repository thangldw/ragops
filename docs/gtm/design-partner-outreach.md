# Design-partner outreach pack

The owner-authorized public invitation is live in
[GitHub Discussion #5](https://github.com/thangldw/ragops/discussions/5).
The copy below remains reusable for direct, reviewed outreach; no direct
messages or emails have been sent automatically.

## Ideal design partner

- A team already operating a RAG or agent pilot.
- At least one recurring quality or release-regression problem.
- Willing to use synthetic/redacted traces first.
- A technical owner and business workflow owner can attend discovery.

## Short outreach message

Subject: Offline release evaluation for your RAG/agent pilot

I’m building RAGOps, an open-source local evaluation and red-team harness for
RAG and agent systems. It turns a team’s quality, latency, cost, citation, and
safety expectations into a versioned release decision.

I’m looking for 3–5 design partners who already have a pilot and can share a
small synthetic or redacted failure set. The first engagement is an offline
60-minute discovery session followed by a reproducible scenario and benchmark;
no production credentials or customer data are required.

In return, I’ll provide the scenario contract, regression report, and rollout
recommendation. I’m specifically looking for feedback on where the workflow
does or does not replace existing notebooks and manual review.

## Discovery agenda

1. Current workflow and release decision owner.
2. Recent failure/regression and business consequence.
3. Trusted evidence, permissions, and prohibited actions.
4. Baseline quality, latency, cost, and operational budgets.
5. Smallest synthetic/redacted evaluation set.
6. Pilot success and stop conditions.
