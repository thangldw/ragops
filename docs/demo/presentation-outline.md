# Ten-minute presentation outline

## 1. Problem — 60 seconds

AI teams can demo quickly but cannot consistently prove a changed RAG or agent
is safe and useful enough to release.

## 2. Product thesis — 60 seconds

RAGOps turns customer and engineering expectations into a versioned evaluation
contract, reproducible evidence, and an explainable go/no-go decision.

## 3. Workflow — 90 seconds

Show scenario → recorded traces → deterministic/plugin evaluators → absolute
and regression gates → PR-ready report.

## 4. Live demo — 180 seconds

Run the accepted Japanese troubleshooting baseline, compare a regressed build,
open the failed cases, and explain why release is blocked.

## 5. Architecture and trust — 90 seconds

Explain the local-first dependency-free core, portable schemas, optional
plugins, security boundary, and why the reference RAG stays outside the core.

## 6. Product potential — 90 seconds

Present the open-core boundary, design-partner plan, hosted collaboration layer,
and honest limitations.

## 7. Close — 30 seconds

RAGOps is the evidence layer between an AI change and a production decision.
