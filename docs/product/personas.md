# Product personas

## AI engineer — primary user

Ships RAG or agent changes and needs a fast, reproducible answer to: “Did this
change improve the system without creating a quality, cost, latency, or safety
regression?” Today this evidence is scattered across notebooks and dashboards.

## Engineering lead — economic buyer and gate owner

Owns production reliability and wants release criteria that can be reviewed in
a pull request, explained after an incident, and reused across teams.

## Forward Deployed Engineer — design partner

Needs to turn ambiguous customer expectations into an evaluation contract,
prove pilot readiness, and present a defensible go/no-go recommendation.

## Security and governance reviewer — policy stakeholder

Needs traceable evidence for sensitive-data leakage, excessive agency, and
approval violations without having to understand every prompt or framework.

## Explicit non-personas for the first product wedge

- Consumers looking for a hosted chatbot.
- ML research teams benchmarking foundation models in isolation.
- Teams seeking a full LLM orchestration framework.
